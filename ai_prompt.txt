NEGATIVE PROMPT
========================================
DO NOT INCLUDE:
- Oversized architecture diagrams dominating the poster
- Huge technology badge walls taking excessive space
- Dense technical infographic appearance
- System architecture board style
- Unreadable tiny text or fake lorem ipsum
- Random placeholder words or gibberish
- Distorted or blurry logos and screenshots
- Low resolution or pixelated graphics
- Crowded cluttered layout with no white space
- Childish cartoon design or clip art
- Inconsistent icon styles mixing different design systems
- Irrelevant stock photos or generic images
- Wrong or mismatched technology logos
- Overdecorated busy background with excessive patterns
- Excessive text blocks or long paragraphs
- Poor alignment or inconsistent spacing
- Low contrast making text hard to read
- Broken or distorted typography
- Generic template appearance
- Unprofessional poster composition
- Too many boxes and containers
- Corporate software diagram board look
- Technical manual aesthetic
- Neon colors or rainbow gradients
- 3D effects or heavy shadows
- Watermarks or sample text overlays
- Comic Sans or unprofessional fonts
- All caps body text
- Underlined text (except links)
- Diagonal or circular text
- Photo backgrounds behind text
- Texture overlays reducing readability
- Excessive borders around everything
- Misaligned elements breaking the grid
- Inconsistent color usage
- Amateur design elements
- Cluttered footer with too much information
- Missing white space and breathing room
